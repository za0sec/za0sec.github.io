One day, my plan is to create a cybersecurity company to help all enterprises in the world providing them security solutions.

If you are starting in cybersecurity, well, thats me while im writing this. Im assuming you are asking, how do I learn to hack?
Let me tell you, as far as I am, its fascinating. Learning cybersecurity will not only open you doors to work some day,
but it will help your daily issues. Let me give you a tiny but helpfull example:

Imagine that you are in a webpage that contains extremely important info that you need rigth now, BUT, the index.html is down.
Not knowing anything of web pentesting will make you close the page. Now, im going to show you a simple tool that will simplify this example:

This tool is called WFUZZ and it lets you (among other features) scan all files in the page.
Well, and now you are asking, why tf do I need to scan all files and directories?
Thats an easy question to answer. In those scanned directories you may find the file that contains your information.
I know... if an advance pentester is reading this, he is laughing. Maybe it was a dumb example but it works to show a tiny feature.

While writing and reading this "article", I noticed that I didnt answer the question, how do I learn to hack? 
I dont think im capable of answer that question but what I can tell you is:

-Be constant.
-Search 4 courses.
-I love my career and university but it will not teach you cybersecurity.
-Investigate (80% of your knowledge will come from this point)
-Enjoy, learning its an enjoyable moment.
-Im joking its hard and painfull but when you achieve the results its cool.
